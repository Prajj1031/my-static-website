BMW History

The Origins of BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) was founded in 1916, with the idea of manufacturing aircraft engines. The company went on to diversify into automobile production, becoming a global leader in the luxury car market. Over the years, BMW has established itself as a symbol of engineering excellence, innovation, and luxury.

BMW's First Automobile

In 1928, BMW acquired Fahrzeugfabrik Eisenach, which allowed them to begin manufacturing their first car, the BMW 3/15. This marked the beginning of their journey into the automotive world.

Iconic Models

Some of BMW's most iconic models include the 328 Roadster, the BMW 3 Series, and the BMW X5. Each of these vehicles has helped solidify BMW's reputation as a manufacturer of high-performance luxury cars.
